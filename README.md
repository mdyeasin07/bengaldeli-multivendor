# Bengaldeli --- Mutlivendor Ecommerce Website

A multi-vendor e-commerce website is a digital platform designed to serve a dual purpose: to facilitate the sale of products and services by multiple vendors and to provide consumers with a diverse and convenient online shopping experience.
